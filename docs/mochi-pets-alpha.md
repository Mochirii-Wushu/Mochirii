# Mochi Pets Alpha

Mochi Pets is a closed Mochirii playtest for approved guild members.

In this first alpha, testers enter one shared 3D guild room, create a curated
character, meet Lirabao, and care for the guild pet together. The tester
password opens the playtest page. The shared guild room is closed to approved
testers only. Saved play requires Mochirii member sign-in, tester approval, and
accepted playtest terms.

Approved testers care for Lirabao together in the same shared room.

## What Members Can Try

- Open the playtest page with the tester password.
- Sign in as a Mochirii member for saved play.
- Choose one of the curated character presets.
- Enter the Jade Lantern room.
- Move around on desktop, wave, and use simple social signals.
- Approach Lirabao and send care interactions.
- Leave, return, and confirm character and Lirabao progress are still there.

## Playtest Boundaries

- The live page stays behind the tester-password wall during alpha.
- All alpha progress has no real value.
- Alpha progress is only for testing and cannot be redeemed, transferred, or
  promised outside this closed playtest.
- Avatar uploads, multiple rooms, and mobile-specific play are not part of this
  first test.
- The room may pause or reset while the alpha is being improved.

## Ready To Invite Testers

Before inviting testers, confirm:

- The page asks for the tester password before showing the room.
- The correct tester password unlocks the playtest page.
- Signed-in approved testers can enter the shared room.
- Testers without approval or accepted terms are blocked.
- Two approved testers see separate characters in the same room.
- Both testers can care for the same Lirabao.
- Character and Lirabao progress remain after reload, logout, and login.
- Public copy stays Mochirii-branded and clearly says no real value.

## If The Room Pauses

If the game room is unavailable after deployment, keep the tester page online
and show a clear playtest-paused message instead of loading the room.

Do not open the page publicly during alpha.
