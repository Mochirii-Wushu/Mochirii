# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep collaboration respectful, focused, and safe for guild members.

Expected behavior:

- Use welcoming and clear language.
- Respect differing experience levels and communication styles.
- Keep feedback specific to the work.
- Avoid sharing private member information.
- Escalate security or safety concerns privately.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private member, account, or security information.
- Deliberate disruption of issues, pull requests, or deployments.
- Attempts to bypass authentication, moderation, or repository protections.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove contributions that violate this code. Repeated or serious violations may result in loss of repository access or guild-facing collaboration privileges.
