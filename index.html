Ranks

Soft Wind New arrival

New to Mochirii. Learn the culture, meet the people & settle in. Courtesy & a minimum of 1k guild activity are the only requirements.

Mist Petal Finding footing

Begins engaging in chat & activities. Shows patience, respect & a willingness to learn.

Initiate Time based

Member for at least one full month. The only rank granted purely by time.

Mochi Blossom Active member

Participates, groups when possible & adds to the community’s daily rhythm with a steady, friendly presence.

Veteran Consistency

Member for at least three months. Active on most days & reliably helpful. No leadership duties required.

Elite Execution helper

Frequently helps with content & questions. Known for accurate guidance, reliability & calm conduct.

Petal Keeper Culture steward

Welcomes new members, reinforces guild norms, de-escalates minor issues & flags concerns early to officers.

Dharmapala Officer

Leads instances & progression content. Teaches systems, mentors members & prevents drama.

Hall Leader Council

Strategic council that oversees officers, resolves escalated disputes & protects long-term guild stability.

Vice Leader Highest honor

Top responsibility & trust. Represents Mochirii externally, manages diplomacy & ensures continuity of leadership.

About / How to Join

Quiet, cozy guild. Mochirii welcomes pretty people from all over the world to a yummy Cupcake home. No pressure, be sweet like frosting, support each other at all times. 30-day Cupcake nap ok, if you let us know.

  • New & returning players are welcome.
  • Questions are encouraged, ego is not.
  • We aim to please, achieve & remain friendly.

Joining is simple:

  1. Join our Discord (link below).
  2. Introduce yourself (short & simple is perfect).
  3. Request an invite in game.

Note: Promotions are based on observable actions, not requests. If something is missed, we try to fix it ASAP.

Rules / Charter Highlights

  • Respect first: speak with patience & good faith.
  • Help when you can: teach, guide & share knowledge without condescension.
  • Keep conflict small: resolve issues privately, escalate only when needed.
  • Represent Mochirii well: your conduct reflects the guild.
  • No rank excuses bad behavior: titles don’t outrank principles.
  • Consistency is key: steady contribution matters most.

Contact / Join

Ready to join Mochirii?

Start with Discord, then we’ll coordinate your in-game invite. Keep it simple: introduce yourself, mention your playstyle & what you want to learn or contribute.
