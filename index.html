Guild Values

Four pillars that keep Mōchirīī cozy even when the world boss is not.

Sweetness

Calm

Resolve issues quietly. Escalate only when needed. Drama stays outside the pavilion.

Craft

Consistency

Steady is Strong

Ranks

From Soft Wind to Vice Leader, each step is earned through presence, kindness, and steady effort.

Soft Wind

New arrival

Learn the culture. Meet the people, settle in. Courtesy and a minimum of 1k guild activity are the only requirements.

Mist Petal

Finding footing

Begins engaging. Shows patience, respect, and a willingness to learn.

Initiate

Time-based

Member for one month. The only rank granted purely by time.

Mochi Blossom

Active member

Participates. Groups when possible, adds to the community’s daily rhythm with a steady, friendly presence.

Veteran

Consistency

Three months minimum. Active on most days and reliably helpful. No leadership duties required.

Elite

Execution helper

Accurate guidance. Frequently helps with content and questions. Reliable, calm conduct.

Petal Keeper

Culture steward

Welcomes new members. Reinforces norms, de-escalates issues, flags concerns early to officers.

Dharmapala

Officer

Leads content. Teaches systems, mentors members, prevents drama.

Hall Leader

Council

Strategic oversight. Guides officers, resolves escalations, protects long-term stability.

Vice Leader

Highest honor

Top trust. Represents Mōchirīī externally, manages diplomacy, ensures continuity of leadership.

Rules / Charter Highlights

Clear and kind, gently aligned. Harmony held, not tightly confined.

Kindness • Respect • Good manners
  • Respect first: speak with patience and good faith.
  • Help when you can: teach, guide, share knowledge without condescension.
  • Keep conflict small: resolve privately, escalate only when needed.
  • Represent Mōchirīī well: your conduct reflects the guild.
  • No rank excuses bad behavior: titles never outrank principles.
  • Consistency is key: steady contribution matters most.

Promotions

Promotions follow observable actions, not requests. Misses get corrected quickly.
